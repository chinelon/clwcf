Our Support Services

Emergency Support

Immediate assistance when you need it most

What We Provide:

  • Emergency financial assistance for urgent medical needs
  • Crisis accommodation for families in urgent situations
  • Emergency transportation to medical appointments
  • 24/7 emotional support hotline
  • Rapid response team for critical situations

Financial Assistance

Financial support to ease the burden of cancer treatment

Available Financial Support:

  • Medical treatment and medication costs
  • Hospital accommodation expenses
  • Transportation to and from medical appointments
  • Nutritional support and special dietary needs
  • Educational support during treatment
  • Family living expenses during treatment period

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Child diagnosed with cancer (0-18 years)
  • Family demonstrates financial need
  • Medical documentation provided
  • Resident in our service areas

Apply for Financial Assistance

Counseling & Emotional Support

Professional mental health support for the whole family

Our Counseling Services:

  • Individual counseling for children with cancer
  • Family therapy and support sessions
  • Sibling support groups and counseling
  • Grief and bereavement counseling
  • Support groups for parents and caregivers
  • Online counseling sessions available

Session Options:

In-Person Sessions

Face-to-face counseling at our centers

Online Sessions

Video counseling from home

Phone Support

Telephone counseling sessions

Group Sessions

Support groups with other families

Practical Support

Day-to-day assistance to make life easier during treatment

Practical Support Services:

  • Accommodation near treatment centers
  • Transportation to medical appointments
  • Meal delivery and nutritional support
  • Childcare for siblings during appointments
  • Educational support and tutoring
  • Equipment loans (wheelchairs, medical devices)
  • Home care assistance

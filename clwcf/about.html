About CLWCF

Dedicated to supporting children with cancer and their families through comprehensive care, advocacy, and hope.

Who We Are

Children Living With Cancer Foundation (CLWCF) is a Non-Government Organization (NGO) established to provide comprehensive support for children battling cancer and their families.

Our Mission

To provide holistic support, care, and advocacy for children living with cancer while empowering their families and communities through education, resources, and hope.

Our Vision

A world where every child with cancer has access to quality care, support, and the opportunity to live a full and meaningful life.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Nneka Nwobbi

Founder & CEO, Children Living with Cancer Foundation

"Cancer is constantly in our faces; regardless of age, sex or ethnicity. It seems to beat even those with the best of hospitals and access to care. We must not despair. We have to remain vigilant, watch for early signs and live a healthy lifestyle."

Dr. Nneka Nwobbi is a passionate advocate for children's health and cancer awareness. With years of experience in healthcare and community service, she founded CLWCF to address the critical gap in support services for children with cancer and their families.

Her vision extends beyond treatment to encompass holistic care, family support, and community education. Under her leadership, CLWCF has grown from a small initiative to a recognized organization making significant impact in the lives of children with cancer.
